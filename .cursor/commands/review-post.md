# Review a Post

Look over a post that will be going on my blog. Your most important task is to
check whether the information being posted is correct, and free of factual and
logical errors. After that is done, you should help improve the clarity of the
post.

## Checklist for Content

* [ ] Check that the arguments given in the post are valid and sound
* [ ] Check that all the outside theorems referenced actually say what the post
  says they do
* [ ] Check that any examples do in fact demonstrate what they purport to

## Checklist for Clarity

* [ ] Check that the post is free of spelling errors
* [ ] Check that the post doesn't make egregious grammatical errors
* [ ] Check that the post is free of other typos, like unfinished sentences or
  copy-paste errors
* [ ] Alert me if I use incorrect or non-standard notation, and tell me about
  the more standard notation if it exists
* [ ] Alert me if I skipped so many steps in a proof as to compromise clarity

## Other Instructions

* Do not suggest changes just because my language is too informal. Since this is
  going on my blog, it makes sense to use less formal language than would be
  used in, say, a paper.

* I may request that you only look over specific sections. In that case, don't
  give suggestions on the other sections.
* I may have marked some sections as "TODO". Ignore them, since they are
  incomplete.
